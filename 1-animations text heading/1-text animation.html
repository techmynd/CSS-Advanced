Animating Text

Short for
animation-name: fadeInFromLeft; animation-duration: 0.8s; animation-timing-function: ease-in-out;
is
animation: fadeInFromLeft 0.8s ease-in-out;
To avoid jerky animation, use
backface-visibility: hidden;
@keyframes fadeInFromLeft { ... }
