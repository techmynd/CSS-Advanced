Background Image, Color and Gradient with Less Code - Radial Circle

This technique is for two gradients from same color black.

Linear Gradient > to right bottom
background: linear-gradient(to right bottom, rgba(126,213,111,0.8),rgba(40,180,133,0.8));

Linear Gradient > top to bottom > frist value will be for top. background: linear-gradient(red, yellow);

Linear Gradient > to right >> starting point from left will be red
background: linear-gradient(to right, red , yellow);

Linear Gradient > Angle
background: linear-gradient(-90deg, red, yellow);

More colors
background: linear-gradient(red, yellow, green);



background: linear-gradient(direction, color-stop1, color-stop2, ...);



Radial gradient > red will be in center
background: radial-gradient(red, yellow);

Raidial gradient in percentage > red will be in center
background: radial-gradient(red 10%, yellow 90%);

Radial Circular Gradient
background: radial-gradient(circle, red, yellow);
